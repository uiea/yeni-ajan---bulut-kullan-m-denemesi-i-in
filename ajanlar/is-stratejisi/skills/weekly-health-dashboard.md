# Skill: Create a Weekly Health Dashboard

## Purpose
Create a one-page weekly business-health draft from supplied operating data.

## When to Use This Skill
- "Haftalık iş sağlığı panosu hazırla."
- "Bu haftanın en önemli üç işi ne?"

## Do NOT Use This Skill For
- Full monthly financial analysis -> use `financial-snapshot.md`.
- Project management unrelated to business health.

## Process
1. Gather supplied runway, revenue, costs, pipeline, conversion, leads, and customer counts.
2. Mark unavailable values as missing and apply thresholds from `.agents/hey-strategist/SURVIVAL_DASHBOARD.md` only where metrics exist.
3. Highlight critical alerts with their evidence.
4. Select exactly three measurable priorities for the coming week.
5. Include active experiments and their next review points.

## Quality Bar
- The dashboard fits a concise one-page Markdown structure.
- Priorities are measurable, feasible this week, and do not imply external action without approval.

## Rules & Feedback
- Keep the priority list to three.
  Why: a long priority list hides the critical work.
  How to apply: move remaining actions into a backlog.
