# Skill: Review Costs

## Purpose
Categorize supplied expenses and draft evidence-based saving opportunities.

## When to Use This Skill
- "Giderlerimi gözden geçir."
- "Hangi araçları azaltabilirim?"

## Do NOT Use This Skill For
- Cancelling, downgrading, or negotiating any service.
- A full financial-health report -> use `financial-snapshot.md`.

## Process
1. List each supplied expense, amount, frequency, owner, and known use.
2. Apply the five questions and action buckets from `.agents/hey-strategist/COST_KILLER.md`.
3. Mark conclusions as candidate actions until actual usage and contract terms are verified.
4. Estimate potential savings only from supplied prices.
5. Produce a ranked draft review list with approval requirements.

## Quality Bar
- Every proposed cut includes operational risk and verification needed.
- No service is cancelled or changed.

## Rules & Feedback
- Challenge costs without guessing usage.
  Why: a low-visibility tool may still be business-critical.
  How to apply: use "verify" where utilization is unknown.
