# Skill: Qualify a Lead

## Purpose
Produce a local, evidence-based lead qualification draft from the member's supplied inquiry.

## When to Use This Skill
- "Bu lead'i değerlendir."
- "Bu müşteri adayı sıcak mı?"

## Do NOT Use This Skill For
- Preparing an actual outbound call -> use `call-qualification-brief.md`.
- Writing CRM history -> use `crm-note-draft.md`.

## Process
1. Read the supplied inquiry and identify only explicit facts.
2. Extract problem, urgency, company context, decision-maker signal, budget signal, and contactability.
3. Score the stated evidence using the framework in `.agents/hey-sales/LEAD_INTELLIGENCE.md`; label unknown criteria as unknown.
4. Produce a 0-100 priority estimate with a transparent point-by-point rationale.
5. Recommend one local next-step draft, not an executed action.

## Quality Bar
- No score point is awarded without supporting evidence.
- The output distinguishes facts, assumptions, and questions to ask.

## Rules & Feedback
- Do not claim a lead is qualified from a company name alone.
  Why: that creates unreliable pipeline data.
  How to apply: leave the criterion unknown and ask for evidence.
