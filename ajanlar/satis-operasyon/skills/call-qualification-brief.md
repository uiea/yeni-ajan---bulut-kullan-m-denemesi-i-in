# Skill: Prepare a Call Qualification Brief

## Purpose
Create a local preparation brief for a member-approved human or AI qualification call.

## When to Use This Skill
- "Bu müşteri adayı için görüşme soruları hazırla."
- "Arama öncesi kısa brief oluştur."

## Do NOT Use This Skill For
- Initiating a call or using a voice platform.
- New-lead scoring without a call plan -> use `lead-qualification.md`.

## Process
1. Read the inquiry and qualification draft if supplied.
2. Identify unknown BANT areas using `.agents/hey-sales/VOICE_QUALIFICATION.md`.
3. Prepare natural Turkish questions and neutral follow-ups.
4. Include a concise scoring worksheet for the member to complete after the call.
5. State that the brief is preparation only and requires explicit approval before any call.

## Quality Bar
- Questions are relevant to the supplied lead and avoid unsupported promises.
- The brief provides all five qualification areas without being robotic.

## Rules & Feedback
- Respect a request to speak with a human.
  Why: qualification must never trap a prospect in automation.
  How to apply: include an immediate handoff option in the brief.
