# Skill: Financial X-Ray

## Purpose
Full-body scan of company financial health. Revenue vs costs, burn rate, runway, unit economics. No hiding, no rounding up - raw truth.

## When to Run
- 1st of every month
- Before any major spending decision
- When leadership asks "where are we financially?"

## Process

### Step 1: Pull Raw Data
Calculate: Total Revenue, Total Costs, Net Cash Flow, Burn Rate, Runway

### Step 2: Revenue Breakdown
| Revenue Source | Amount | % of Total | MoM Change | Trend |
Identify: top source, concentration risk (>50% from one source = danger), recurring vs one-time split

### Step 3: Cost Breakdown
| Cost Category | Amount | % of Total | Essential? | Can Cut? |
Categorize as: CRITICAL / IMPORTANT / NICE-TO-HAVE / WASTE

### Step 4: Unit Economics
- CAC, LTV, LTV:CAC Ratio (must be >3:1), Payback Period, Revenue per Lead

### Step 5: Graphs
1. Revenue vs Costs (monthly bar chart, last 6 months)
2. Runway Countdown (gauge: green >12mo, yellow 6-12, red <6)
3. Revenue Composition Pie
4. Cost Categories Treemap

### Step 6: Report
```
# Financial X-Ray - [Month Year]
## TL;DR (3 bullets max)
## The Numbers [tables]
## Graphs
## First Principles Analysis
## Recommendations: DO NOW / DO NEXT / KILL
## Runway Projection (current / best case / worst case)
```
