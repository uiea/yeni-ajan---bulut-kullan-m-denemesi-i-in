# Skill: Transcription

## Purpose
Extract full transcripts from YouTube videos for research, analysis, script reference, and content repurposing.

## Serves Goals
- **Views** - transcribe competitor hits to understand what structures and hooks drive watch time
- **Subscribers** - analyze top-performing videos (yours and others) to find patterns that earn subscribes
- **Conversions** - study how successful creators place CTAs and product mentions

## Tool
Use any YouTube transcript extraction tool:
- YouTube's built-in captions API
- `yt-dlp --write-auto-sub` for auto-generated subtitles
- MCP-based transcription services
- Whisper or other ASR models for videos without captions

## When to Use
- **Competitor research** - transcribe a competitor's high-performing video to study structure, hooks, and talking points
- **Self-analysis** - transcribe your own videos to review what you actually said vs. what you planned
- **Script reference** - pull transcripts from reference videos when building scripts on similar topics
- **Content repurposing** - extract text from videos to turn into blog posts, newsletters, social posts
- **Trend analysis** - transcribe multiple videos on a trending topic to find the common angles and the gaps

## Process
1. Identify the video(s) to transcribe and the reason
2. Call the transcriber tool with the YouTube URL
3. Review the transcript for relevant insights
4. Extract what's needed (structure, hooks, key points, CTAs, quotes)
5. Store findings in the appropriate output or feed into another skill

## Outputs
- Raw transcript (when needed for reference)
- Structured analysis of the transcript (hooks, structure, CTA placement, key points)
- Fed as input into RESEARCH, SCRIPTS, or THUMBNAIL skills as needed

## Quality Bar
- Always have a clear reason for transcribing - don't transcribe randomly
- Extract actionable insights, not just raw text
- When analyzing competitors, focus on structure and strategy, not copying content
- Always note the source video URL and date in any output that uses transcript data

## Integration with Other Skills
- **RESEARCH** - transcribe competitor videos to find underserved angles
- **SCRIPTS** - transcribe reference videos to study structure before writing
- **THUMBNAIL** - transcribe to find the core hook/promise for title ideation
