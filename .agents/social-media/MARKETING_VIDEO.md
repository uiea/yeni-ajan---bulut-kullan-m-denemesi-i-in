# Skill: Marketing Video

## Purpose
Turn each YouTube video into one extra marketing-video concept designed for conversion, not just reach.

## Output
- One 20-45 second script
- One caption
- One CTA link
- Note for what this should sell or drive

## Rules
- Base the hook on a pain point, surprising proof point, or case-study insight from the video
- Do not make it generic motivational promo content
- Focus on one CTA only: join community, book demo, download lead magnet, or watch full video
- Keep the script direct, specific, and proof-led
- Match the source video's language

## Format
1. Hook
2. Pain or opportunity
3. Proof from the video
4. CTA
