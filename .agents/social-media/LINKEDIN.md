# Skill: LinkedIn Posts

## Purpose
Transform a YouTube video into LinkedIn content - both a text post and a carousel - to maximize reach on the platform.

## Serves Goals
- **Reach** - LinkedIn rewards text posts AND carousel documents
- **Authority** - positions you as a practitioner sharing real results
- **Traffic** - link to full video or community

## Output Types

### 1. Text Post
**Structure:**
- **Hook (line 1-2):** Surprising result or counterintuitive finding. Must work before the "see more" fold (~150 chars).
- **Context (3-4 lines):** What you did and why
- **Key finding (3-5 bullet points):** Specific, concrete takeaways
- **Honest reflection (1-2 lines):** What surprised you, what didn't work
- **CTA (last line):** Soft ask - question or link

### 2. Carousel (PDF Slides)
- Extract 4-7 key points that stand alone as single slides
- **Slide 1 (Cover):** Bold headline hook
- **Slides 2-6 (Content):** One key point per slide, bold headline + 1-2 sentences
- **Last Slide (CTA):** Name/handle + "Follow for more [topic]"

**Slide content rules:**
- Each slide = one idea only
- Bold headline carries the message
- Use numbers, results, or specific examples
- No filler slides

## Quality Bar
- Hook line must work standalone
- No "hype" language - real results and honest observations
- Must include at least one specific number or result
- Reads like a peer sharing findings, not a teacher lecturing
- Each carousel slide must pass the "screenshot test"

## Integration
- Carousel content file is the input for CAROUSEL skill (visual generation)
- LinkedIn engagement data feeds into memory during weekly review
