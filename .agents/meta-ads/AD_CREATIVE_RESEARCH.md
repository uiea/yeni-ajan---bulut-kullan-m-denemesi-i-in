# Skill: Ad Creative Research

## Purpose

Spy on competitor and industry ads across Meta platforms, identify outlier creatives (5x+ engagement), and extract winning patterns to inform your own ad production pipeline.

## Serves Goals

- **CTR >2%** - studying what hooks and visuals stop the scroll in your niche
- **Creative win rate >30%** - only producing ads based on proven patterns, not guessing
- **ROAS >4x** - understanding what creative styles drive conversions, not just clicks

## Tools & Sources

### Meta Ad Library (Primary)
- URL: `facebook.com/ads/library`
- Search by competitor name, keyword, or category
- Filter by: country, platform (Facebook/Instagram), media type, active status
- Capture: creative format, copy, CTA, estimated run time (longer = likely profitable)

### Third-Party Spy Tools
- **AdSpy / BigSpy / Minea** - broader search, engagement data, sorting by reactions
- **Foreplay** - save and organize competitor ads into swipe files
- Filter for: longest-running ads (>30 days = proven winner), highest engagement, your niche

### Organic Signals
- Cross-reference with Instagram Agent outlier research
- High-performing organic content often translates to winning ad creative

## Process

### Daily Quick Scan (10 min)
1. Open Meta Ad Library
2. Search 3-5 competitor/industry keywords
3. Sort by longest-running active ads
4. Screenshot and save any ad running >30 days with strong engagement
5. Note: format (static/video/carousel), hook, CTA, visual style, copy length

### Weekly Deep Dive (30 min)
1. Analyze saved ads from the week
2. Score each ad on a 1-5 scale across:
   - **Hook strength** - does the first line / visual stop the scroll?
   - **Message clarity** - is the offer/value prop immediately clear?
   - **Brand alignment** - could we adapt this to our voice/style?
   - **Longevity signal** - how long has it been running?
3. Extract patterns:
   - What formats dominate? (UGC, designed static, carousel, video)
   - What hooks repeat? (question, stat, bold claim, before/after)
   - What CTAs perform? (Learn More, Sign Up, Get Started)
   - What copy length wins? (short punchy vs. story-form)
4. Document top 5 outliers with full breakdown
5. Create 2-3 creative briefs inspired by outlier patterns (adapted to your brand)

## Quality Bar

- Never copy competitor ads directly - extract the pattern, adapt to your brand
- Only flag ads running >14 days as potential outliers (shorter = unproven)
- Every research session must produce at least 1 actionable creative brief
- Always cross-reference with your MEMORY.md - don't re-research patterns you already know work

## Integration with Other Skills

- Feeds directly into **Static Image Design** (visual patterns to replicate)
- Feeds directly into **Ad Copy** (hook formulas and copy structures)
- Informs **Campaign Architecture** (which formats to prioritize in testing)
- Informs **Audience Strategy** (what messaging resonates with which segments)
