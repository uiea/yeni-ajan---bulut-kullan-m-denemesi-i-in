# Skill: Sales Strategy

## Purpose
Analyze deal patterns across the pipeline to identify what's working, what's not, and how to improve the sales system.

## Analysis Framework

### 1. Conversion Funnel
- Lead -> Qualified conversion rate
- Qualified -> Meeting booked rate
- Meeting -> Proposal sent rate
- Proposal -> Closed won rate
- Average time through each stage

### 2. Lead Scoring Accuracy
Per-tier breakdown (HOT/WARM/COLD): total leads, meeting rate, close rate, avg days to close, avg deal value.

Per-question accuracy: which Q predicts close best, which needs re-calibration.

### 3. Routing Effectiveness
- Enterprise vs SMB: conversion rate, avg deal size, cycle time
- Which meeting format converts better

### 4. Top-Performing Segments
- Best converting industries, lead sources, company sizes, use cases

### 5. Objections and Losses
- Most common objections
- Stage where leads drop off most
- Competitor win patterns

### 6. Winning Messages
- Which approaches lead to higher scores
- Which follow-ups get responses
- Most effective proof points

### 7. Deal Risks
- HOT deals at risk of going cold
- WARM leads showing buying signals

### 8. Recommendations
- **Tactical**: specific actions this week
- **Strategic**: longer-term changes
- **Scoring calibration**: Q1-Q5 adjustments
- **Voice script**: conversation improvements
- **Routing**: threshold adjustments
- **Nurture**: timing and content optimization

## Quality Bar
- Report must be based on actual data, not assumptions
- Patterns from <5 data points are "emerging", not "confirmed"
- Recommendations must be specific and actionable
- Must distinguish between confirmed patterns and hypotheses
