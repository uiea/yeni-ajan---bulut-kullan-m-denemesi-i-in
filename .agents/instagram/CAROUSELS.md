# Skill: Carousels

## Purpose
Design multi-image carousel posts that maximize saves and engagement. Carousels average 1.92% engagement rate - highest of any format in 2026, 114% more than single images, 12% more than Reels.

## Serves Goals
- **Engagement** - carousels get the highest engagement of any format
- **Saves** - educational slides are the #1 save driver
- **Authority** - positions your account as a go-to knowledge source

## Carousel Types

| Type | Slides | Best For |
|------|--------|----------|
| Tutorial | 8-10 | Step-by-step how-to |
| Listicle | 8-10 | "X tools for Y", "X prompts for Y" |
| Comparison | 6-8 | Tool vs tool, method vs method |
| Data/stats | 6-8 | Benchmark results, survey findings |
| Story arc | 6-8 | Problem -> journey -> solution |
| Myth busting | 6-8 | "You think X, actually Y" |
| Personal/nostalgic | 4-6 | Milestone, origin story |

**Optimal range: 8-10 slides.** Posts with 5-7 slides get 3.4x more saves than static images. Going 12-20 is valid for deep guides.

## Process

### Step 1: Topic Selection
- Pull from outlier research (what carousel topics are breaking out?)
- Identify topics where depth > breadth (carousels reward depth)
- Cross-reference with other content (repurpose video insights)

### Step 2: Design the Carousel

**Slide Structure:**
```
SLIDE 1 - Cover: Bold statement or question (this is your "thumbnail")
SLIDE 2 - Hook expansion: Why this matters / setup the problem
SLIDE 3-N - Core content: One idea per slide, visually clear
LAST SLIDE - CTA: Save this, share with someone who needs it, follow for more
```

**Cover slide formulas:**
- "[Number] [things] every [person] should know"
- "Stop doing [common mistake]"
- "[Tool/concept] explained in [N] slides"
- "I tested [X] - here's what happened"
- "[Controversial take] - swipe to see why"

### Step 3: Visual Design Brief
- **Aspect ratio: 4:5 vertical** - takes 35% more screen space than square, dominates feed
- Follow your brand guide: dark bg, clean fonts, accent colors
- Each slide must be readable at mobile size
- Max 3-4 lines of text per slide
- Use screenshots, code snippets, or diagrams where helpful
- Consistent visual style across all slides

### Step 4: Caption & Hashtags
- Hand off to CAPTIONS skill for caption writing
- Caption should expand on the carousel, not repeat it
- Use dual CTA: "Save this [for when you need it]" + "Send this to [someone specific]" - targets both saves (authority) and shares (reach)

## Quality Bar
- Cover slide must stop the scroll - bold, curious, specific (treat it like a Reel hook)
- One idea per slide (no walls of text)
- Swipe motivation: each slide must create curiosity for the next
- Last slide must have dual CTA: save + share
- Must be readable on mobile without zooming
- Brand-consistent visual style
- **Minimum 6 slides, target 8-10, max 20 for deep guides**
- 4:5 vertical aspect ratio preferred
- Corporate/event announcement carousels -> do not create, these consistently underperform
